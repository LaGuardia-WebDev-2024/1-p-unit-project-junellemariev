The Big Pooty

What is a Pooty?

Pooty is my dog
He likes scratching walls
But Pooty is not big,
He's not even a foot tall!
He came into my life my sophomore year.
He came as a surprise to everyone here.
And although he's not big, his booty sure is
The Big Booty Pooty, what a perfect fit!

Why is he The Pooty?

Because he is the only one! The only Pooty in the world to live with me and my family.
